DIAGRAM Center: Code Sprint 2018

CodeSprint Goals

  • Identify, create, and enhance tools that make STEM education more accessible for students with disabilities and learning differences.
  • Knowledge share - engage subject matter experts (e.g., educators, parents, end-users) and technologists to collaborate and exchange ideas.
  • Identify potential STEM tools and projects for continued development.
  • Expand the DIAGRAM Accessible code repository to build awareness!

  • Math Editor
